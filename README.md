# ISO/IEC 27001 Information Security Management Program  
## MoveSynch Technologies (PulsePro Platform)

## Overview
This project presents a **comprehensive ISO/IEC 27001–aligned Information Security Management System (ISMS) proposal** for **MoveSynch Technologies**, a health-technology company developing *PulsePro*, a wearable fitness and health-tracking platform.

The proposal evaluates MoveSynch’s **business expansion risks**, designs a **standard-based security governance framework**, and provides a **practical roadmap** for achieving regulatory compliance, risk reduction, and operational resilience.

---

## Business Context
**Company:** MoveSynch Technologies  
**Product:** *PulsePro* – fitness & health-tracking wearable  
**Data Types:**  
- Health metrics (heart rate, blood pressure, sleep, activity)
- Location (GPS)
- User accounts & social data

**Strategic Growth Drivers**
- AI-driven personalized health plans
- Social media integration
- Third-party insurance partnerships
- Cloud migration & European market expansion

---

## Objectives of the Security Program
- Protect **confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA)** of sensitive health data
- Establish **risk-based security governance**
- Align security with **business strategy and leadership**
- Achieve **ISO/IEC 27001 readiness**
- Ensure compliance with:
  - GDPR
  - Australian Privacy Act
  - Health data protection obligations
- Foster a **security-aware organizational culture**

---

## Key Security Risks Identified
The assessment identified major risks across technical, organizational, and regulatory domains:

- **Sensitive health data exposure**
- **Cloud security misconfiguration**
- **Third-party insurance & supplier risk**
- **Social media integration threats**
- **AI data integrity & poisoning risks**
- **Supply-chain security weaknesses**
- **Logistics and operational disruption**
- **Rapid organizational restructuring risks**

---

## Why ISO/IEC 27001
ISO/IEC 27001 was selected as the foundation because it:

- Provides a **holistic people-process-technology framework**
- Enables **continuous risk assessment and improvement**
- Strengthens **customer trust and brand reputation**
- Supports **regulatory compliance** across jurisdictions
- Scales with organizational growth and innovation

---

## Key ISMS Components Designed

### 1️⃣ Leadership & Governance
- Executive-level security policy
- Defined roles, responsibilities, and authorities
- Integration of security into business processes

### 2️⃣ Risk Assessment & Management
- ISO 31000–aligned risk framework
- Risk identification, analysis, evaluation, and treatment
- Continuous reassessment for evolving threats

### 3️⃣ Operations Planning & Security
- Change management controls
- Secure system operations
- Capacity planning and monitoring
- Third-party security oversight

### 4️⃣ Organizational Structure & HR Security
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Separation of duties
- Security awareness and onboarding training
- Insider threat risk reduction

### 5️⃣ Regulatory Compliance
- GDPR-aligned consent management
- Data minimization & retention controls
- Incident response & breach notification plans
- Third-party compliance assurance

### 6️⃣ Communication Management
- Internal security awareness programs
- External stakeholder and customer notifications
- Transparent third-party data handling communication

### 7️⃣ Documentation & Control
- Centralized ISMS documentation
- Secure access controls
- Versioning, backups, and retention policies
